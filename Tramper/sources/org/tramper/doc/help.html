Manage the speech synthesizer

Open the speech synthesizer control panel using one of the 3 ways:
  • Click on the "Speech synthesizer" button to the top of the window with your mouse
  • Use the shortcut "Alt + S" by using your keyboard
  • Say the speech command "Synthesizer" in your microphone
